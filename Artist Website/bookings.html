Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For best availability, we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance. For major events or festivals, 3-6 months in advance is ideal.

Do you travel outside of Pretoria?

Yes, I perform nationwide across South Africa. Travel expenses are included in the pricing for regional and nationwide packages.

What is your payment process?

A 50% deposit is required to secure the booking, with the remaining 50% due 7 days before the event. Payments can be made via bank transfer or other agreed methods.

Can you perform cover songs?

Yes, I can include cover songs in the performance. Let me know your preferences during the booking process.
