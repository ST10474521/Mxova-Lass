About Mxova Lass

The story behind the music

My Musical Journey

Mxova Lass is a 19-year-old Hip Hop and Amapiano artist from Pretoria, South Africa. Born in January 2006, he represents the voice of a new generation of South African musicians blending traditional sounds with contemporary urban music.

Growing up in the vibrant streets of Pretoria, Mxova was exposed to diverse musical influences from an early age. The rhythmic beats of Amapiano and the raw storytelling of Hip Hop shaped his unique sound that captures the essence of South African youth culture.

Musical Style

My music is a fusion of gritty Hip Hop flows and infectious Amapiano beats. I draw inspiration from my surroundings, telling authentic stories from the streets while creating danceable tracks that get people moving.

Achievements & Recognition

  • Featured Artist - SA Hip Hop Awards (2023)
  • Top 10 - Metro FM Music Awards (2024)
  • Breakthrough Artist - Pretoria Menlyn Perfomance (2024)
  • Collaborations with some SA Upcoming Artists (2022-2025)

Musical Influences

Amapiano Roots

Drawing from the rich Amapiano tradition of South Africa, log drums and melodies with contemporary production.

Hip Hop Storytelling

Inspired by the lyrical depth of Hip Hop, telling authentic stories from the streets of Pretoria and beyond.

African Sounds

Incorporating diverse African musical elements to create a sound that represents the continent's vibrant musical heritage.
