Mxova Lass is a 19-year-old Hip Hop and Amapiano artist from Pretoria, South Africa. Born in January 2006, he's quickly making a name for himself with his unique blend of gritty hip hop flows and infectious amapiano beats.

His music captures the energy of South African youth culture while telling authentic stories from the streets of Pretoria.
